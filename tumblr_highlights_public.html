Visualizing the Reblogs of tumblr Posts

Ever wonder how a post spreads across tumblr? I did, so I built this tool to interactively visualize the reblogs of a tumblr post. Each node / dot is a tumblr user, and the vertices / lines connecting the nodes show how a post can spread from user to user through reblogs.

Below, you can explore three examples of reblog structures using the posts that I've picked from my tumblr, languageramblings. Or, you can also provide a post URL to visualize its reblog structure! Just be warned that if the post has a lot of notes (500+), it may take a few minutes...
